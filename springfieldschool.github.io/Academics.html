Our Academic Programs

Primary School (Creche Nursery,Kindergarten)

Our primary school program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking. We nurture curiosity and creativity while providing a balanced curriculum that includes arts, science, and physical education.

Middle School (Primary 1-6)

The middle school years are an exciting time for growth and exploration. At Springfield School, we emphasize a strong academic core while encouraging students to discover their individual interests through electives such as technology, foreign languages, and performing arts.

High School (J.H.S 1-3)

Our high school program prepares students for Senior high School(S.H.S)and beyond. With a rigorous curriculum that includes advanced placement (AP) courses, a focus on leadership, and opportunities for internships, Springfield School graduates are ready to excel in higher education and their chosen careers.

Beyond the Classroom

Extracurricular Activities

Springfield School offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, from sports teams to clubs like debate, robotics, and the arts. These opportunities allow students to develop leadership skills and pursue their passions.

STEM Programs

Our STEM programs are designed to inspire the next generation of innovators. Students have access to modern labs, cutting-edge technology, and opportunities to participate in competitions and research projects.

Academic Achievements

Springfield School is proud of its students' academic achievements. Our students consistently perform above national averages in standardized testing, and many go on to attend prestigious universities worldwide.
