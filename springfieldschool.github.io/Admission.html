Welcome to the Admissions Process

We are thrilled that you are considering Springfield School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be as simple and transparent as possible. Below, you will find all the information you need about the application process, important dates, and requirements for different grade levels.

Admissions Process

Our admissions process consists of the following steps:

  • Step 1: Submit an application form
  • Step 2: Attend a school tour (optional but recommended)
  • Step 3: Submit required documents (report cards, birth certificate, etc.)
  • Step 4: Attend an interview and/or entrance test (if applicable)
  • Step 5: Receive admissions decision and next steps

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: September 1st, 2024
  • School Tour: September 10th - October 20th, 2024
  • Application Deadline: November 15th, 2024
  • Entrance Exam (if applicable): December 1st, 2024
  • Admissions Decision: January 10th, 2025

Eligibility Criteria

We welcome students from all backgrounds who meet the following criteria:

  • Age Requirements: Must meet the minimum age requirement for the grade level by September 1st of the school year.
  • Academic Requirements: Previous academic records should demonstrate a strong academic foundation.
  • Behavior: Students must have a positive disciplinary record from their previous school.
